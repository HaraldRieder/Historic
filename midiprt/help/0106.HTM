Actually, the note numers I use are their MIDI numbers with a number base of 12 (not 10 = decimal and not 16 = hexadeciamal).

Thus I also use the new term "dodecime" instead of the traditional "octave".

The base of 12 is not intrinsic to music. It is only a common approach to the "real" notes, which actually are innummerable. The MIDI specification and the MIDI File Printer are based on this common approach.
