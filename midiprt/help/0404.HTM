4.4 ST Emulator

With ST Emulator 1.6 several effects could be observed, that could not be reproduced on Magic 6 or TOS 4:

  • More than 20 points font size can not be set.
  • 3D ellipses are not correctly drawn (dashed upper line is wrong) in palette modes, but are drawn correctly in direct color mode.
  • vst_height() leads to wrong results when printing with the GDOS emulation. Therefore the MIDI file printers uses vst_point() only. (Also some metafile viewers seem to have problems with vst_height().)
  • The activation of the "hot close box" window flag leads to pixel garbage on the screen. Therefore this flag is always off.
