2.9 Hot Keys

The hot keys in this chapter are not documented in any menu.

Whenever a character is underlined, the corresponding control can be triggered by Alternate+character.

On Atari TOS a double-click to an arrow button of a slider positions it to the corresponding end. In the filter window use Contol+Tab and Enter to switch all items on or off.

On Windows a selected slider or choice can be manipulated with arrow and page keys. Control+Q leaves the application in a way, that the currently open files will be remebered and re-opened at the next application start.
