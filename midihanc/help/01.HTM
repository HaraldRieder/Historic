Mad Harry's MIDI Enhancer

1 Introduction

The MIDI Enhancer allows you to play microtonal music on conventional synthesizers supporting the MIDI poly mode (multi mode) and on GS compatible MIDI instruments (consult the manual to find out whether your instrument is GS compatible - GM compatibility does not include GS compatibility).

With the MIDI Enhancer you get a number of ready-to-use scales, like meantone, arabian and natural ones. You can change the scale while playing, and you can change the harmonic context ("harmonizing note") without changing the scale type while you are playing (e.g. you keep the scale type "natural" but switch its base from C# to G#). The scale switches are smooth and take into consideration the current context, unlike brute scale switches as performed even by expensive commercial MIDI tools.

Furthermore you get some additional "kit features" like MIDI string sending and volumes mapping, that might be of interest for you - or not. You even may use these kit features without needing to enter the world of microtonalism.
